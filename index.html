Privacy Policy

Data Collection and Storage

Local Storage: All data you save using this extension is stored locally on your device using chrome.storage.local (for Chrome) or browser.storage.local (for Firefox, when available). This includes any custom autofill entries you create.

No Data Transmission: The extension does not send any data to external servers. All operations are performed locally within your browser.

Third-Party Services

Water.css, Google Fonts, and jsDelivr: The extension uses Water.css, Google Fonts, and jsDelivr for styling purposes. While I do not collect any personal data, these third-party services may collect information such as IP addresses, browser type, operating system, and user interactions.

  • Google Fonts: The Google Fonts API logs the details of the HTTP requests, including the requested URL, IP address, and user-agent, to serve the appropriate fonts. This data is used for aggregated analysis of font usage. No cookies are set or logged by Google Fonts. More details can be found in the Google Fonts Privacy Policy.
  • jsDelivr: jsDelivr logs visitor IP addresses, user agent, and referring URLs for performance monitoring, security, and aggregated analytics. This information helps improve the service and ensure its reliability. More details can be found in the jsDelivr Privacy Policy.

Data Security

Local Data: Your data remains on your device and is not shared with anyone outside of this extension. However, be aware that malicious extensions, browser exploits, or physical access to your device could potentially compromise this data. Always ensure your browser and extensions are from trusted sources and keep your device secure.

Export/Import: You can export your data for backup purposes and import it back when needed. Ensure you store these backups securely as they contain your saved data in plain text.

User Responsibilities

Sensitive Data: Avoid saving sensitive information in the extension. Although your data is stored locally, exported files can be accessed by anyone with access to them.

Regular Backups: Regularly export your data to prevent loss in case of browser data corruption or accidental deletion.

Liability: I am not liable for any data loss or compromise resulting from user actions, browser vulnerabilities, software bugs, or other factors beyond my control. While every effort is made to ensure the extension functions correctly, bugs may occur. Users should regularly export their data to prevent potential loss. It is your responsibility to use the extension securely and keep your data and devices protected.

Changes to This Policy

I may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be communicated through the extension update notes or via a notification within the extension itself. Users are encouraged to periodically review this privacy policy for the latest information.
